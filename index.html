Synthwave Taxi

Synthwave Taxi is an 80's synthwave inspired driving game where you try to avoid other cars by jumping over them or swapping lanes. Ride into the sunset, don't crash, and enjoy the synthwave.

Press Left Arrow (or A) to swap left, press Right Arrow (or D) to swap right, and press spacebar to jump.
